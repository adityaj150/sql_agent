# Company Refund Policy

## Hardware Refunds
Any hardware purchases can be refunded within 30 days of the original purchase date. 
The item must be in its original packaging.

## Software Refunds
Software subscriptions cannot be refunded once the billing cycle has started, but you can cancel at any time to prevent future charges.
